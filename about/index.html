Why a platform

Models find vulnerabilities. A platform proves them.

Frontier models are remarkable at finding possible vulnerabilities. But a model is not a pentesting platform. Proving exploitability, staying safe in production, orchestrating agents at scale — that is the platform.

  • Proof, not noise

    Every finding ships with a working proof of concept. If it can't be exploited, it never reaches you.

  • Safe by design

    Non-destructive probes, full audit trails, production-safe validation. The swarm never breaks what it tests.

  • Orchestration at scale

    A coordinator keeps the swarm aligned — no duplicated work, no gaps in coverage, however many agents you run.

  • Model-agnostic

    Routing to the best model for each task. No lock-in, no single point of failure, always the sharpest reasoner available.
